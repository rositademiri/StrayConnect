Adoption requirements

Age: You must be at least 18 years old to adopt a pet from our shelter. This ensures that the adopter is legally responsible for the animal's care
Identification: A valid government-issued ID, such as a driver's license or passport, is required for the adoption process.
Residence verification: Adopters should provide proof of residency, such as a lease agreement, mortgage statement or utility bill, to ensure they have a permanent home for the pet.
Financial Stability: Adopters may be asked about their financial situation to ensure they can afford the costs associated with pet ownership.
Home visit: Our shelter conducts home visits to assess the living environment and ensure it is suitable for the pet.
Pet History: Adopters may be asked about their experience with pets and any current or past pets they have owned.
